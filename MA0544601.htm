MA0544601: Digital Geometry Processing
2016.02-2016.06

This course provides an introduction to digital geometry processing, a subfield of computer graphics. This course will cover basic mathematical foundations for studying 3D surfaces from a discrete differential geometric standpoint and present the full geometry processing pipeline, including surface representation, 3D data scanning, registration, surface reconstruction, mesh smoothing, parameterization, and surface deformation. The second part of this course will introduce some basic maching learning methods, and mainly focus on its applicatons in geometry processing.

References: Polygonal Mesh Processing, The Elements of Statistical Learning
