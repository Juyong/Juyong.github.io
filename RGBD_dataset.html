RGB-D Facial Dataset under Pose Variation


Introduction:
This dataset contains RGB-D facial images in different poses. It is used in the following paper:
- Luo Jiang, Juyong Zhang, Bailin Deng. Robust RGB-D Face Recognition Using Attribute-Aware Loss. arXiv: 1811.09847.

This RGB-D facial datasert is captured by PrimeSense camera in the same indoor scene and contains 24839 RGB-D images of 952 identities, where each identity also has corresponding attributes including gender, ethnicity, and age. The new dataset contains only young Asians with 30% male and 70% female, and covers pose with yaw ranging from left 90° to right 90°.
